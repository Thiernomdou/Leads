# 10 Pain Points - Psychologue TCA Lyon

These emotional pain points are used throughout the website (homepage, CTAs, blog intros) to create empathetic connections with potential patients.

## Pain Points

1. **"Je me sens prisonnier(e) de mes comportements alimentaires"**
   - Feeling trapped in a cycle of restriction, binging, or purging
   - Related: loss of autonomy, dependency on food behaviors

2. **"La honte m'empeche d'en parler"**
   - Shame as the #1 barrier to seeking help
   - Related: secrecy, isolation, fear of judgment

3. **"J'ai deja essaye seul(e), sans y parvenir"**
   - Failed self-recovery attempts leading to hopelessness
   - Related: need for professional specialized support

4. **"Mon rapport a la nourriture affecte toutes mes relations"**
   - Social and relational impact of eating disorders
   - Related: avoiding meals with others, conflict around food

5. **"Je ne me reconnais plus dans le miroir"**
   - Body dysmorphia and disconnection from self-image
   - Related: identity crisis, loss of self

6. **"La nourriture occupe toutes mes pensees"**
   - Obsessive food thoughts consuming mental energy
   - Related: inability to concentrate, mental exhaustion

7. **"J'ai peur des consequences sur ma sante"**
   - Health anxiety about physical consequences of the disorder
   - Related: dental damage, heart issues, fertility

8. **"Je ne sais pas si ce que je vis est un TCA"**
   - Uncertainty about whether one's experience qualifies as a disorder
   - Related: minimization, denial, subclinical presentations

9. **"J'ai peur de grossir si je commence un suivi"**
   - Fear that therapy will lead to weight gain
   - Related: weight-focused thinking, misunderstanding of therapy goals

10. **"Je veux m'en sortir mais je ne sais pas par ou commencer"**
    - Desire for change without knowing the first step
    - Related: overwhelm, decision paralysis, need for guidance
